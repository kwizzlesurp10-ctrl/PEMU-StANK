## üéØ Mission

PEMU-StANK is an open-source toolkit designed to promote consumer awareness through ethical, transparent, and legal means. We believe in empowering consumers with information and tools to make informed purchasing decisions without resorting to deception, manipulation, or illegal activities.

## ‚öñÔ∏è Core Principles

This project is built on five non-negotiable ethical pillars:

### 1. **Transparency**
- All methods and intentions are publicly disclosed
- No hidden agendas or deceptive practices
- Open-source codebase for community review
- Clear documentation of all features and functionality

### 2. **Legality**
- Strict respect for copyright and intellectual property
- Compliance with platform terms of service
- No trademark infringement or brand impersonation
- Adherence to data protection regulations (GDPR, CCPA, etc.)

### 3. **Consent**
- Users explicitly opt-in to all activities
- No unauthorized data collection or sharing
- Clear privacy policies and data handling procedures
- Respect for individual autonomy and choice

### 4. **Attribution**
- Proper citation of all sources and research
- Credit to original creators and contributors
- Acknowledgment of intellectual property rights
- Transparent about data sources and methodologies

### 5. **Sustainability**
- Built for long-term positive impact
- Community-driven development and governance
- Focus on education over exploitation
- Collaborative approach to consumer advocacy

## üõ†Ô∏è Proposed Features (Roadmap)

This repository is a framework for ethical consumer activism tools. Below are proposed features that align with our principles:

### Phase 1: Educational Resources
- **Dark Pattern Detection Guide**: Documentation of manipulative UX patterns
- **Psychology of Pricing**: Educational materials on pricing psychology
- **Supply Chain Transparency Database**: Crowdsourced ethical sourcing information
- **Consumer Rights Reference**: Country-specific consumer protection laws

### Phase 2: Browser Tools
- **Price History Tracker**: Monitor price fluctuations over time
- **Reviews Analyzer**: Statistical analysis of review authenticity
- **Carbon Footprint Calculator**: Estimate environmental impact of purchases
- **Alternative Product Finder**: Suggest ethical alternatives to products

### Phase 3: Community Platform
- **Verified Reviews System**: Blockchain-based purchase verification
- **Ethical Vendor Directory**: Community-curated marketplace alternatives
- **Consumer Discussion Forums**: Safe space for sharing experiences
- **Advocacy Campaign Coordinator**: Organize legitimate consumer movements

### Phase 4: Developer Tools
- **API for Transparency Data**: Public API for ethical sourcing information
- **Open Standards**: Propose industry standards for transparency
- **Integration Libraries**: SDKs for adding transparency features to apps
- **Audit Tools**: Help developers identify dark patterns in their own products
